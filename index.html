About Me

“Learning never exhausts the mind.” ― Leonardo da Vinci

In 2016 I was inspired to change the course of my life. Whilst traveling, I observed many new opportunities that could be available to me. Whilst there, I successfully applied to the AWS:restart program and began upskilling in AWS services.
