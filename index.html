About Me

“Learning never exhausts the mind.” ― Leonardo da Vinci

In 2016 I was inspired to change the course of my life. Whilst traveling, I observed many new opportunities that could be available to me. I decided to relocate from Poland to the UK to begin building my skills and career, most recently at Amazon for 7 years. Whilst there, I successfully applied to the AWS:restart program and began upskilling in AWS Cloud.

After working in customer service/amazon warehouse role, I stumbled upon the exciting world of cloud technology. It's incredible how it's revolutionizing businesses everywhere, so I decided to make it my thing! I've been honing my skills with Amazon Web Services (AWS) through programs offerd by Generation UK and Ireland, and QA Ltd. These courses have been fantastic in teaching me the ins and outs of AWS services like EC2, S3, and Lambda. Additionally, I've delved into Linux system management and network fundamentals. Understanding how these components interact with cloud environments has been instrumental in my journey. Now, I'm well-versed in cloud infrastructure, deploying applications, and ensuring security measures are top-notch. I enjoy leveraging AWS tools to craft efficient solutions for various challenges. I'm eager to apply my knowledge as a cloud engineer and contribute to innovative projects. Always up for learning and staying updated with industry trends, let's chat and explore how we can team up to make a difference in the cloud world!

In my professional life I am guided by the following principles:

  • Be a good human being
  • Never stop learning
  • Always look for the golden mean
